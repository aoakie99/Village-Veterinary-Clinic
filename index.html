Village Veterinary Clinic, PLLC

Village Veterinary Clinic offers comprehensive veterinary care for all small animals in Westfield, New York. Our experienced veterinarians are committed to providing the highest quality care and surgical procedures.

Our Services

WELLNESS EXAMS

Yearly exams are important for keeping your pet happy and healthy. A thorough nose to toes exam goes a long way towards helping to maintain our furry friends well-being for many years to come!

MICROCHIPPING

A quick, easy, and virtually painless procedure implanting a microchip the size of a piece of rice. This procedure offers a permanent way of identifying your pet in the event that they ever become lost or stolen.

SURGERY

Our clinic offers a full range of surgery services including; spays and neuters, mass removals, entropion repair, cystotomy, gastrotomy/enterotomy, gastropexy and much more!

EUTHANASIA

We understand that nothing is easy during this difficult time. We are here every step of the way, from peaceful euthanasia to cremation services, and to help wherever we can. Make your pet’s last moments peaceful and comforting with our compassionate team.

VACCINATIONS

Keeping a pet up to date on their vaccinations can sometimes mean the difference between life and death. We offer all routine vaccines as well as those for Lyme disease, Influenza and Leptospirosis.

DENTAL CARE

Dental health is one of the most crucial parts of your pet's overall health, comfort and longevity. Stop in to schedule a dental exam to determine if your pet would benefit from a cleaning.

RADIOLOGY

Digital radiography (x-ray) captures black and white, two-dimensional images of the interior of your pet. It is used to evaluate a variety of pet health conditions such as:

  • Broken Bones
  • Foreign Objects
  • Lungs
  • Heart
IN-HOUSE LABORATORY

Our newly updated on-site laboratory provides rapid and accurate diagnostic evaluations of blood, urine, and fecal material. We can also perform allergy blood testing for both environmental and food allergies.

Angle Fund
The Angel Fund is a fund that helps defray the cost of medical and emergency care expenses of pets surrendered to or abandoned at the Village Veterinary Clinic, PLLC. These animals are treated and then adopted out. Your contribution to the Angel Fund of any amount would be greatly appreciated.

Meet the Staff

Jessica Wilkins-Hazenberg
CEO, DVM

Dr. Mark McConnon
DVM

Dr. McConnon is a 1981 graduate of The Ohio State University and completed his internship at Cornell University. He served as the New York State Veterinarian from 1997 until his retirement in 2014.
